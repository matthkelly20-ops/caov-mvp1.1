## Current Summary

CAOV stands for College Athletics Operations Venture.

The current MVP wedge is baseball away-trip operations.

The MVP should be an **Away-Trip Operations Workspace** that turns an uploaded or manually entered schedule into structured away-trip workspaces.

The workspace should show:

- What is known.
- What is missing.
- What needs confirmation.
- Who owns each item.
- What goes into the final itinerary/trip packet.

## Product Promise

> We help college athletics teams execute away trips with less chaos, less manual work, and fewer missed details.

## Key Product Insight

Real baseball travel workflows show that away-trip logistics are not just itinerary fields. They are request/confirmation workflows involving host communication, internal finance, services, pass lists, practice/lift access, and final artifact generation.
